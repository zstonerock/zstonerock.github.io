why stonerock

Personal trainers are a dime a dozen anymore, so I'm sure you're asking what sets me apart. I'm not your stereotypical trainer, meaning you won't see me in any magazine covers anytime soon for my physique. I share a lot of the same daily struggles and obstacles that you do. What I bring is a scientific approach to help develop a plan for YOU. I don't buy into the "bro-science" techniques. Whether you want to train with me 7 days a week or once a month I'm always available as a resource to guide you along the way to achieving your goals. I will hold you to a high standard. I give each of my clients 100% so I expect the same effort in return. I don't do this to get rich, I do this because it's a passion and I want to share that passion with others.

My name is Zach Stonerock and I'm a certified personal trainer in the Central Ohio area.
